Daft Punk

All began with the music...

– Origin: Paris, France
– Members: Guy-Manuel de Homem-Christo, Thomas Bangalter

Worlwide acclamaided french band, formed in 1993. Thanks to his second album “Discover” they had the idea of create something special: a serie of videoclips of their songs working together with Leiji Matsumoto, one of the most prolifics anime artist in Japan. Putting all together we got “Interstella 5555: The 5tory of the 5ecret 5tar 5ystem” a movie where music and animation are the main protagonists!.

They are also well known for their mysterious appearances wearing robot helmets. They may be aliens..?, well at least they will be sharing stage with some of them in this unique experience, where they will perform the music from Interstella 5555. Let's listen to them on Summer 2018!
